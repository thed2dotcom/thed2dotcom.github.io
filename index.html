Building Better Brands

There’s something pretty magical about walking into a store and seeing your product on the shelf for the first time. It’s goose-bump worthy, and we’ve experienced it many times with our clients. We love taking great ideas and bringing them to life, and we love building brands for people all around the world to enjoy. Each member of our team has spent several years working at large corporations that do this on a global level, and we’ve each chosen to now use our talents and experience to work with small to mid-size startups to help more people realize their dream of launching a brand. If you’re looking for that goose-bump feeling, you’re in the right place.

Services

Go-to-Market
Strategy

Start small and scale up. We are firm believers that choosing the right geography and customers to launch in is just as important as getting the product and packaging right.

Brand Design
& Strategy

Know who you are AND who you’re not. We help clients solidify their brand positioning and identity so that there’s always a strong foundation to build upon. We work on logo and package design to develop brand guidelines that create a consistent look and feel.

Social Media
Engagement

You have to engage with your consumers. Most of our clients don’t have the big bucks to spend on traditional advertising like TV and radio, so we work with them to craft and execute an online strategy that will attract and engage current and potential consumers.

Key Account
Sales Calls

They can’t buy it if they can’t find it. We’ve called on and have relationships with small regional and large national chains in every segment of business across the US, and we work with clients to carefully select the right ones for where they are in their brand life cycle.

Distribution
Management

Location, location, location. There’s a wide variety of distributors and territories to launch and grow a brand, and we help our clients identify the right ones. We have relationships all across the US with large national and small regional distributors to help introduce and get new brands set up for success.

Innovation
Pipeline

Remain relevant. Building a brand often involves developing new products and line extensions. We work with clients to help outline emerging trends, competitor activity and consumer needs to build and bring to life a long-term pipeline of innovation that continues to grow the brand.

Our Work

In our former lives, we were fortunate enough to work on some of the biggest brands in the world, including Pepsi, Mtn Dew, Lipton, Aquafina, Starbucks, Gatorade, Heineken, Dos Equis and a whole lot more. We’re using that experience every day to help our current clients, and these are just a few we’re proud to call partners.

Get In Touch

If you already have a brand you’re trying to grow or thinking of launching something new, shoot us a note so we can set up some time to chat to see if we can help. Seeing your brand on shelf for the first time is one of the most exciting experiences, and we’d love to be a part of your dream.
